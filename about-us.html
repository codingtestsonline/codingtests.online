About Us

Welcome!

At codingtests.online, we are passionate about making English language learning accessible, effective, and enjoyable for everyone. Founded by language education enthusiasts, our platform offers hundreds of free English tests designed to help learners at all proficiency levels improve their skills.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower English learners worldwide by providing high-quality, reliable assessment tools that help them track their progress and identify areas for improvement. We believe that language proficiency opens doors to educational and professional opportunities, and we're committed to supporting learners on their journey.
